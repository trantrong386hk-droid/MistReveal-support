常见问题 / FAQ
如何开始使用 / How do I get started?
注册账号后，在「探索」页面输入你的出生年月日和时辰，App 会根据八字为你分析命格并生成专属灵魂伴侣。

After signing up, enter your birth date and hour on the Explore page. The app analyzes your BaZi chart and generates your personalized soul companion.
生成次数用完了怎么办 / What if I run out of generations?
每位用户有一次免费生成机会。邀请好友注册即可获得额外生成次数，或在应用内解锁订阅。

Each user gets one free generation. You can earn extra by inviting friends, or unlock a subscription within the app.
灵犀伴侣是什么 / What is the Lingxi companion?
灵犀是根据你八字命格专属生成的 AI 对话伴侣，具有独特的人格、五行属性和说话风格，支持持续深度对话。

Lingxi is an AI companion generated uniquely from your BaZi profile, with a distinct personality, elemental traits, and conversational style.
如何删除我的账号 / How do I delete my account?
前往「我的」→「设置」→「删除账号」，按提示确认即可永久删除账号和所有相关数据。

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account and confirm. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data.
App 支持哪些语言 / What languages are supported?
目前支持简体中文和英文，可在「我的」→「设置」中切换。

Currently Simplified Chinese and English are supported. Switch in Profile → Settings.
忘记密码怎么办 / I forgot my password
在登录页面点击「忘记密码」，输入注册邮箱，我们会发送重置链接到你的邮箱。

Tap "Forgot Password" on the sign-in screen and enter your registered email. A reset link will be sent to you.
数据安全吗 / Is my data safe?
你的数据通过 Supabase 安全存储，传输全程加密。AI 图像生成通过服务端代理完成，客户端不接触原始 API 密钥。详见我们的隐私政策。

Your data is securely stored via Supabase with encrypted transmission. AI generation is handled server-side. See our Privacy Policy for details.
