简体中文

1. 引言

欢迎使用缘起（MistReveal）（以下简称"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私，并致力于保护您的个人信息。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的数据。

2. 我们收集的信息

您主动提供的信息：

  • 注册信息：电子邮件地址、密码
  • 出生信息：出生年月日、出生时辰（时辰/小时精度）、出生城市
  • 个人资料：昵称、性别、头像（可选）
  • 聊天内容：您与 AI 灵犀伴侣的对话记录
  • 用户手册：您主动填写的个人偏好信息（可选）

自动收集的信息：

  • 位置信息：城市级别的粗略地理位置（用于星图匹配功能，需获得您的授权）
  • 设备信息：操作系统版本、设备类型（仅用于调试）
  • 使用数据：功能使用频率、错误日志（不含个人身份信息）

3. 我们如何使用您的信息

  • 根据八字命理计算您的命格特征，生成专属 AI 灵魂伴侣形象和对话人格
  • 提供灵犀 AI 对话服务，并根据亲密度进化对话风格
  • 在星图功能中匹配与您命格共振的附近用户（仅显示城市级别位置）
  • 发送账号相关通知（如验证码、重置密码）
  • 改善应用功能和修复错误
  • 处理应用内购买

4. 信息存储与安全

您的数据通过 Supabase（美国）安全存储，传输全程使用 TLS 加密。AI 图像生成（阿里云百炼、火山引擎即梦）通过我们的服务端代理完成，API 密钥不存储在客户端。我们采取行业标准的技术和管理措施保护您的数据，但无法保证绝对安全。

5. 第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务，各方均有其独立的隐私政策：

  • Supabase：数据库与身份验证（supabase.com）
  • 阿里云百炼：大语言模型服务
  • 火山引擎即梦：AI 图像生成
  • Google Sign-In：可选的第三方登录（google.com）
  • Apple Sign-In：可选的第三方登录
  • Apple App Store：应用内购买处理（apple.com）

6. 数据共享

我们不会出售您的个人信息。仅在以下情况共享数据：

  • 经您明确同意
  • 向上述第三方服务提供商提供提供服务所必要的最少数据
  • 法律法规要求

星图功能中，您的城市级别位置和五行属性（不含具体出生时间）会对其他用户的星图可见。

7. 数据保留与删除

您可以随时在应用内「我的」→「设置」→「删除账号」永久删除账号和所有相关数据，包括聊天记录、生成图像和命理档案。删除操作不可撤销，将在 30 天内完成彻底清除。

8. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下未成年人。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。如发现儿童已提供个人信息，请联系我们，我们将及时删除。

9. 您的权利

根据适用法律，您有权：访问、更正、携带、限制处理或删除您的个人数据。请通过下方联系方式与我们联系。

10. 政策更新

我们可能不定期更新本隐私政策。重大变更将通过应用内通知告知您。继续使用本应用即表示接受更新后的政策。

11. 联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系：

电子邮件：support@mistack.dev

English

1. Introduction

Welcome to MistReveal (缘起) ("the App"). We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information: email address, password
  • Birth information: date of birth, birth hour (hour precision), birth city
  • Profile: nickname, gender, avatar photo (optional)
  • Chat content: your conversations with the AI Lingxi companion
  • User Manual: personal preferences you voluntarily provide (optional)

Automatically collected information:

  • Location: city-level approximate location for the Star Map matching feature (requires your permission)
  • Device info: OS version, device type (for debugging purposes only)
  • Usage data: feature usage frequency, error logs (no personally identifiable information)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate your BaZi destiny chart and generate a personalized AI companion portrait and personality
  • Provide the Lingxi AI conversation service and evolve dialogue style based on intimacy level
  • Match nearby users with resonating destiny charts on the Star Map (city-level location only)
  • Send account-related notifications (e.g., verification codes, password resets)
  • Improve app features and fix bugs
  • Process in-app purchases

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored via Supabase (United States) with TLS encryption for all transmissions. AI image generation (Alibaba Cloud Bailian, Volcano Engine JiMeng) is handled through our server-side proxies — API keys are never stored on the client. We apply industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, though we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • Alibaba Cloud Bailian: large language model service
  • Volcano Engine JiMeng: AI image generation
  • Google Sign-In: optional third-party login (google.com)
  • Apple Sign-In: optional third-party login
  • Apple App Store: in-app purchase processing (apple.com)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is only shared:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With the above service providers to the minimum extent necessary to deliver the service
  • When required by law

On the Star Map feature, your city-level location and elemental attribute (not your specific birth time) are visible to other users' star maps.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time via Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This includes chat history, generated images, and destiny profiles. Deletion is irreversible and will be fully completed within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Your Rights

Under applicable law, you have the right to access, correct, port, restrict processing of, or delete your personal data. Please contact us using the details below.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact:

Email: support@mistack.dev
